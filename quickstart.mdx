## Overview

Properpost lets you create organized documentation spaces for your projects. You start by signing up, then build your first space, explore the interface, and upload content. Follow these steps to launch your documentation in under 5 minutes.

<Callout kind="tip">
  New to Properpost? Use your GitHub account for seamless signup and repo integration later.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up and log in to access your dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Properpost" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to `properpost.com`. Click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Sign-Up Method" icon="user-plus">
    Select **Continue with GitHub** or enter your email and password. Verify your email if prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Answer basic questions about your project. You land on the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Spaces organize your docs like folders for projects.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, select **New Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, add a description, and choose visibility (public or private).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Space" icon="rocket">
    Click **Create**. Your space appears in the sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Your First Document

Add content to your space via upload, creation, or import.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upload File" icon="upload">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Upload">
        In your space, click **Upload** > **File**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose Document">
        Pick a Markdown file like `README.md`.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Create New" icon="plus">
    Click **New Document**. Use this starter template:

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
    ````mdx
    ---
    title: My First Doc
    description: Welcome to my documentation.
    ---

    ## Introduction

    Start writing here.
    ````

    ```markdown
    # My First Doc

    ## Introduction

    Start writing here.
    ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import from GitHub" icon="github">
    Connect your repo:

    ```bash
    # In space settings, paste your repo URL
    https://github.com/yourusername/myproject
    ```

    Properpost syncs branches automatically.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
